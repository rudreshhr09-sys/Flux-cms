About the Project

Distortion as
Expression.

FLUX explores the boundary between structure and chaos. Each piece in this collection lives through interaction — responding to your touch with liquid GLSL shaders, chromatic aberration, and WebGL-powered distortion effects.

Built with Three.js, GSAP, custom GLSL vertex and fragment shaders, and Lenis smooth scroll. No frameworks. No shortcuts. Pure creative development.
